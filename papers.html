Literature Review & Research Analysis

Abstract:
Recent advancements in IoT-based display systems have enabled real-time data visualization in public environments. This section reviews existing smart display systems and centralized digital boards used in commercial and smart city applications.

1. IoT-Based Smart Display Systems

Several research works demonstrate the use of ESP8266 and ESP32 for real-time data transmission to LED or LCD displays via WiFi. These systems primarily focus on advertisement boards or institutional notice boards.

2. Centralized Monitoring Systems

Cloud-based dashboards integrated with REST APIs allow remote data updates. However, many implementations target industrial monitoring rather than commodity price transparency in local markets.

4. Identified Research Gap

Most existing systems lack:
  • Affordable implementation for rural markets
  • Commodity-focused pricing management
  • Multi-board centralized synchronization for small-scale environments
Our research addresses these limitations by designing a scalable, low-cost, IoT-based centralized digital price board specifically for local bazaars.
